PROJECT 01 · E-COMMERCE

Amazon Sales Data Analysis

Analyzed 32,000+ Amazon records (Jan–Apr 2025) covering Order ID, Product Category, Sales, Profit, Location and Payment Method to uncover revenue drivers and regional trends.

  • Cleaned data: removed nulls, dropped duplicates, extracted date features
  • SQL queries for category-wise revenue & region-wise order analysis
  • Power BI dashboard: Revenue, Profit, Total Orders, Avg Order Value
  • Insight: Electronics + Home Appliances = ~95% of total revenue
  • Insight: Refrigerators, Laptops & Smartphones are top profit drivers
  • Insight: Houston & Miami had the highest order volumes

PROJECT 02 · RETAIL SALES

Motorola Smartphone Sales Analysis

Analyzed 4,000 smartphone transactions with 19,000 units sold to uncover brand performance, monthly sales trends, and customer payment behavior.

  • Removed duplicates, handled missing values, extracted month & weekday features
  • SQL for brand-wise revenue & payment method distribution
  • Power BI: Total Sales ₹769M · Qty 19K · Transactions 4K
  • Insight: Apple, Samsung & Vivo each contributed ~₹160M in revenue
  • Insight: Saturday recorded the highest single-day sales volume
  • Insight: UPI & Debit Card were the most-used payment methods

PROJECT 03 · QUICK COMMERCE

Blinkit Grocery Sales Analysis

Analyzed 19,000+ Blinkit records (Order ID, Product, Category, Sales, Profit, Discount, Quantity, City, Delivery Time) to optimize operations and pricing strategy.

  • Removed nulls, converted delivery time to numeric, standardized categories
  • SQL for city-wise orders & discount impact on sales performance
  • Power BI: Revenue, Profit, Orders, Avg Delivery Time
  • Insight: Dairy & Beverages contribute ~40% of total revenue
  • Insight: Delhi & Mumbai had the highest order frequency
  • Insight: Average delivery time was approximately 8.5 minutes
